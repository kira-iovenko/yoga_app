Yoga Plan

  • Mountain Pose – Stand tall, grounding your feet. Improves posture and balance.
  • Downward Dog – Hands and feet on the floor, hips raised. Stretches spine and hamstrings.
  • Child’s Pose – Kneel and rest your torso forward. Calms the body and mind.
  • Cobra Pose – Lie on your stomach, lift chest with arms. Opens the chest and strengthens the spine.
  • Tree Pose – Balance on one leg with the other foot on your thigh. Builds focus and stability.
